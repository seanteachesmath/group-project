Checkpoint 1.4.16. Problems with "\sech" and "\csch" -- RESOLVED.

When in math mode, for some reason \sech and \csch do not work and you have to enter them a different way. What worked for me so far is using \mathrm{sech} and \mathrm{csch} to make the text to display correctly.

Resolved.

In order to correctly display these, you must create a macro in the <macros> section of the main.ptx file. You do this by adding "\DeclareMathOperator{\sech}{sech}" into the section.
