To align equations, check out the following code,

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} (x+1)^2 &= (x+1)(x+1)\\ &= x^2+x+x+1\\ &= x^2 + 2x +1 \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

Or, perhaps better yet,

\begin{align*} (x+1)^2 &= (x+1)(x+1)\\ &= x^2+x+x+1\\ &= x^2 + 2x +1 \end{align*}

This is the hyperbolic cosecant function, \(\csch(x)\text{.}\)
